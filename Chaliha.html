This creamy Orecchiette Pasta with Carrot Miso Sauce is a flavorful, healthy, and comforting dish—perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • Orecchiette pasta (or any small pasta shape)
  • Carrots (peeled and chopped)
  • White miso paste
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetable broth (or water)
  • Lemon juice
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, or vegan substitute)
  • Fresh parsley or chives (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the orecchiette pasta according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté garlic until fragrant, then add chopped carrots and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add vegetable broth, cover, and simmer until the carrots are soft (about 10 minutes).
  4. Transfer the carrot mixture to a blender. Add miso paste, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Blend until smooth, adding pasta water as needed for a creamy consistency.
  5. Return the sauce to the skillet and toss with the cooked pasta. Adjust seasoning if necessary.
  6. Serve warm, garnished with parsley or chives, and sprinkle with Parmesan if desired.

Tips for Success

  • Use fresh, high-quality miso paste for the best flavor.
  • To save time, prepare the carrot sauce while the pasta cooks.
  • Add a touch of red pepper flakes for a spicy kick.
  • If you’re out of orecchiette, substitute with penne or farfalle pasta.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make this recipe gluten-free?

Yes, simply substitute the orecchiette pasta with gluten-free pasta of your choice.

How do I store leftovers?

Store the pasta in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat gently on the stovetop with a splash of water to loosen the sauce.

Can I freeze the carrot miso sauce?

Yes, the sauce can be frozen for up to 3 months. Thaw in the refrigerator and reheat before tossing with pasta.
