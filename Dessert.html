Vegan Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.95 from 450 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Serves: 2

This vegan chocolate avocado mousse is rich, creamy, and full of flavor. Made with healthy avocados, this dessert is a delicious alternative to traditional chocolate mousse.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients in a food processor or blender until smooth.
  2. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Vegan Banana Bread

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.89 from 500 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 55 mins

Serves: 8 slices

A moist and flavorful banana bread made with ripe bananas and simple vegan ingredients. Perfect for breakfast, a snack, or dessert!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas and mix in coconut oil, maple syrup, and vanilla.
  3. In another bowl, mix dry ingredients, then combine with wet ingredients.
  4. Pour into the loaf pan and bake for 45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.93 from 600 votes

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 12 mins

Total Time: 27 mins

Serves: 24 cookies

Delicious vegan chocolate chip cookies that are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. A perfect treat for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl, then add the wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Form dough into balls and flatten slightly on the baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 12 minutes or until golden. Let cool before serving.

Vegan Cheesecake

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.96 from 750 votes

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 1 hour

Total Time: 1 hour 15 mins (plus chilling time)

Serves: 8

A creamy and rich vegan cheesecake that’s perfect for dessert lovers. Made with cashews and coconut cream for a smooth texture without any dairy.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups cashews (soaked for at least 4 hours)
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup almond flour (for crust)
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil (for crust)
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup (for crust)

Instructions

  1. For the crust, mix almond flour, melted coconut oil, and maple syrup. Press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  2. Blend soaked cashews, coconut cream, maple syrup, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Pour the filling onto the crust and freeze for at least 3 hours before serving.

Vegan Coconut Macaroons

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.91 from 400 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Serves: 12 macaroons

These vegan coconut macaroons are crispy on the outside and chewy on the inside. A sweet treat that’s simple to make and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Scoop the mixture onto the baking sheet and bake for 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
