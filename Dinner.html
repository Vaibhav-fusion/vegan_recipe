Vegan Chili

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.90 from 460 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Time: 40 mins

Serves: 4-6

A hearty and comforting vegan chili packed with beans, vegetables, and spices, perfect for a cozy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot, sauté onions, bell pepper, and garlic until softened.
  2. Add tomatoes, beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, pepper, and broth.
  3. Simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve hot.

Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.85 from 380 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Total Time: 55 mins

Serves: 2

These stuffed sweet potatoes are filled with savory black beans, corn, and a tangy avocado topping, making for a satisfying vegan meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Pierce sweet potatoes with a fork and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix black beans, corn, chili powder, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Once sweet potatoes are tender, slice them open and stuff with the bean mixture. Top with avocado and serve.

Vegan Stir-Fry

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.80 from 400 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 25 mins

Serves: 2-4

A quick and colorful vegan stir-fry loaded with fresh vegetables and tofu, perfect for a nutritious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 block tofu, cubed
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, sauté tofu until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, stir-fry vegetables until tender.
  3. Add tofu back to the pan and stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, and garlic powder. Cook for another 2-3 minutes. Serve hot.

Total Time: 25 mins

Serves: 3-4

These vegan tacos are filled with a tasty mixture of lentils, avocado, and fresh salsa, perfect for Taco Tuesday.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked lentils
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 4 taco shells
  • 1 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. Warm taco shells in the oven or on a skillet.
  2. Fill each shell with lentils, avocado slices, salsa, cilantro, and lime juice.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Vegan Pasta Primavera

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.88 from 450 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Time: 30 mins

Serves: 4

A vibrant and flavorful pasta dish featuring a variety of sautéed vegetables and a creamy plant-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup plant-based cream
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, sauté vegetables in olive oil until tender.
  3. Stir in plant-based cream, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. Toss with pasta and serve immediately.
