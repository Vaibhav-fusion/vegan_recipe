Vegan Buddha Bowl

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Total Time: 40 mins

Serves: 2

This vibrant and nourishing Vegan Buddha Bowl is a perfect combination of fresh veggies, grains, and proteins. Loaded with healthy ingredients like quinoa, chickpeas, and avocado, it’s as delicious as it is satisfying!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas (cooked or canned)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp tahini dressing

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
  2. Assemble the bowl with quinoa, chickpeas, avocado, greens, cucumber, and carrots.
  3. Drizzle with tahini dressing. Serve immediately.

Lentil Soup

★★★★★
4.85 from 280 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Total Time: 45 mins

Serves: 4

This hearty Vegan Lentil Soup is comforting and full of flavor. Packed with lentils, vegetables, and spices, it’s a perfect dish for any season.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, sauté onions, carrots, celery, and garlic for 5 minutes.
  2. Add lentils, vegetable broth, cumin, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer for 30-35 minutes until lentils are tender. Serve hot.

Cook Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Serves: 2

These Vegan Wraps are quick to make, healthy, and packed with fresh veggies, hummus, and avocado, making them perfect for lunch or a snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup hummus
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed greens
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Spread hummus on the tortillas.
  2. Add avocado, greens, carrots, cucumber, and drizzle with lemon juice.
  3. Roll up the tortillas and serve immediately.

Chickpea Salad Sandwich

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 0 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Serves: 2

A protein-packed Vegan Chickpea Salad Sandwich, made with mashed chickpeas, vegan mayo, and fresh vegetables. Perfect for lunch or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and mashed
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayo
  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 4 slices bread

Instructions

  1. Mash chickpeas in a bowl, then mix with mayo, celery, onion, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread the mixture onto slices of bread. Serve immediately.

Total Time: 10 mins

Serves: 2

Enjoy a light and creamy Vegan Caesar Salad made with fresh romaine lettuce, crispy croutons, and a vegan Caesar dressing. A perfect side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup vegan Caesar dressing
  • 1/2 cup croutons
