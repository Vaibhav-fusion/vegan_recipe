Roasted Chickpeas

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.85 from 300 votes

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Serves: 4

These crispy roasted chickpeas are a delicious, healthy snack that's easy to make and full of flavor. Great for munching or adding to salads and bowls!

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Pat chickpeas dry and toss with olive oil and spices.
  3. Spread evenly on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  4. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!

Vegan Energy Balls

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.90 from 250 votes

Prep Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Serves: 12 balls

These Vegan Energy Balls are perfect for a quick snack or post-workout bite. Packed with oats, nuts, and natural sweeteners, they're a healthy, no-bake treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Form the mixture into small balls (about 1 inch in diameter).
  3. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes. Serve and enjoy!

Total Time: 10 mins

This Veggie Hummus Dip is the perfect appetizer or snack! It’s creamy, flavorful, and pairs wonderfully with fresh veggies or pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hummus
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

Vegan Popcorn

Rate this recipe:
★★★★★
4.87 from 150 votes

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 5 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Serves: 2

Vegan popcorn is an easy, healthy snack that's quick to prepare. Top it with your favorite spices or seasonings to make it extra tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Optional toppings: nutritional yeast, smoked paprika, garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add popcorn kernels and cover. Shake the pan occasionally until popping slows.
  3. Remove from heat, season with salt or toppings. Serve immediately.
